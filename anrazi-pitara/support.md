### 2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Does the app work offline?**
Most grammar lessons and stored sets are available offline. Speaking features and ads require an internet connection.

**Are my voice recordings saved?**
No, your voice is used only for real-time speech recognition to help you practice speaking and is not stored by Dholix Technologies.

**I found a mistake in a translation.**
We strive for accuracy! If you find a typo or an incorrect translation, please email us the screenshot so we can fix it in the next update.
