# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 11, 2026**

Dholix Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **Angrezi Pitara – Learn English** mobile application (the "App") available for Android and iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### a. Voice and Audio Data
The App includes speaking exercises and voice-based features. To use these, the App requires access to your device's microphone. 
- **Usage:** Voice data is processed specifically for speech recognition to help you improve your English pronunciation.
- **Storage:** We do not record or store your audio files on our servers. Processing is typically handled via on-device APIs or third-party service providers (like Google/Apple Speech Recognition).

### b. Local Data Storage
We use technologies like `AsyncStorage` to save your learning progress, quiz scores, and settings locally on your device. This data stays on your phone and is not transmitted to our servers.

### c. Device Information and Identifiers
For performance monitoring and advertisements, we may collect non-personally identifiable information such as:
- Device model and version.
- Operating system (Android or iOS).
- Unique device identifiers for advertising.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Google Play Services / Apple App Store:** Facilitate app functionality and updates.
*   **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to show personalized ads. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
*   **Speech Recognition Services:** Depending on your device, voice data may be processed by Google Speech-to-Text or Apple Speech Recognition.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Angrezi Pitara is designed for users of all ages interested in learning English. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe we have collected data, please contact us.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions:
- **Microphone:** To enable Speaking Chat and Speech Exercises.
- **Storage:** To save app data and progress.
- **Internet:** To load advertisements and fetch lesson updates.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
